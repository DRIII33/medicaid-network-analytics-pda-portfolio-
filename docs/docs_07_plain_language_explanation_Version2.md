# Plain Language Explanation – Medicaid Data Dashboard

## Purpose

This dashboard was created to make it easier for both technical and non-technical staff at HHSC to understand how well the Medicaid program is doing at connecting members with health care providers, and to spot any unusual billing or eligibility patterns that may need attention.

## What You Can Learn at a Glance

- **Are members able to see a doctor or specialist when they need to?**
- **Are there enough providers in each area?**
- **Is there evidence of possible billing errors or fraud?**
- **How are services and spending trending throughout the year?**

## Key Points in Non-Technical Terms

- **Green numbers and high percentages mean the program is working as intended.**
- **Red numbers, sudden drops, or extremely high numbers mean issues that may need more investigation.**
- **Tables and charts let you see information by county, provider type, and time period, so you can focus on the places or times that matter to your role.**

## When and How To Use

- For regular program review meetings
- When preparing responses for policy makers or OIG
- To prioritize site visits, audits, or member outreach

**If you notice something odd, use the feedback tools or let your team lead know so it can be investigated further.**

*This plain language explanation is included to support transparency and usability for all HHSC staff and collaborators.*
